ROBERT MILES

Electronic music DJ & Producer


His early life

Robert Miles (really named Roberto Concina) was born in Fleurier - Switzerland in 1969. Miles became proficient at playing the piano during his youth in Friuli, Italy, in the small town of Fagagna, where his family moved when he was still a young boy, and had been in the music scene since 1984. He worked as a DJ in some Italian clubs and private radio networks and in 1990, he used his savings to establish his own recording studio and a pirate radio station.
